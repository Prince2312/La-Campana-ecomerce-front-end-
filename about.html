About La Campana

Discover our story, our passion, and our commitment to authentic Italian cuisine

Our Story

Nestled in the picturesque town of Palampur, La Campana brings the authentic flavors of Italy to the foothills of the Himalayas. Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Bianchi, our restaurant combines traditional Italian recipes with locally sourced ingredients to create a unique dining experience.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where guests could experience the true essence of Italian hospitality. Today, we continue to uphold that vision by focusing on quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and warm, attentive service.
