Our Story

Nestled in the picturesque town of Palampur, La Campana brings the authentic flavors of Italy to the foothills of the Himalayas. Founded in 2010 by Chef Marco Bianchi, our restaurant combines traditional Italian recipes with locally sourced ingredients.
